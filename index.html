An accordion is a UI element that allows users to expand and collapse sections of related content. It's commonly used for FAQs, menus, or organizing information efficiently.

Accordions improve user experience by reducing clutter and making interfaces cleaner. They help users focus on one section at a time without overwhelming them with too much information.
